About Avynera

Avynera is a modern health tracking application designed to help you monitor and improve your overall wellbeing. Track weight, water, sleep, and mood; understand trends; and get helpful context through an AI-powered assistant — with a UI crafted to feel calm, focused, and premium.

Health tracking

🤖

Thoughtful interface

Our mission

We want health tracking to feel simple, intelligent, and accessible. Avynera helps you understand your body with less friction and more confidence — wherever you are on your journey.
