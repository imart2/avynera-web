Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Avynera (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a mobile health tracking application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App and related services connected to our servers. By using Avynera, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Avynera, we may process:

  • Account data: information you provide when registering or signing in (for example email address, username, and authentication credentials managed securely by our backend).
  • Health & wellness data you enter: daily metrics such as weight, water intake, sleep duration, mood, and optional notes — stored to power your dashboard, charts, goals, and insights.
  • Preferences: language selection, daily goal targets (e.g. water, sleep, weight), and notification/reminder settings you configure in the App.
  • Technical & usage data: data needed to operate the service, such as device or app identifiers, timestamps, and diagnostic information when you interact with our API (for example to sync your entries or check premium status).
  • Premium & billing-related data: if you purchase or activate a premium package, our payment partner (e.g. Midtrans) may process payment-related information according to their terms; we may store order identifiers and payment status as needed to unlock premium features.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide core features: logging health data, displaying progress, streaks, reminders, and history.
  • Synchronize data between your device and our servers when you are signed in, so your records stay aligned across sessions.
  • Offer premium features where applicable, such as AI-assisted coaching, analytics & predictions, and AI health chat — which may send relevant portions of your profile or entries to our backend (and, where applicable, to AI services we use) to generate responses.
  • Process premium activation and payment status (including retries or status checks after checkout).
  • Improve reliability, security, and product experience.

Not medical advice: Avynera is for general wellness and informational purposes only. It does not replace diagnosis or treatment by a qualified health professional.

4. Local storage, backup, and PDF (on your device)

The App stores some data locally on your phone for performance and offline access. If you use premium backup features, you may export data (for example as JSON or CSV) or import a backup file; those actions are initiated by you.

Monthly PDF reports (where available) are generated on your device from your local health data. The App does not need to upload that PDF to our servers for it to be created; you choose how to share or store the file.

5. AI processing

Features such as coaching or AI health chat may send text and contextual information (for example your app language and relevant health fields) to our backend. Our backend may use third-party AI providers to generate responses. Do not submit emergency or highly sensitive information you would not want processed for this purpose.

Examples include payment processors (such as Midtrans for checkout in supported flows) and infrastructure or AI vendors. Their use of data is governed by their respective policies in addition to this Policy where they act on our behalf.

7. Data retention & security

We retain information as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data; however, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
